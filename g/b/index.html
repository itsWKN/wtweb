Guide to Creating a Discord Bot

Welcome to our guide on creating a Discord bot!

Step 1: Setting up a Discord Developer Account

  1. Go to the Discord Developer Portal.
  2. Log in or create an account if you haven't already.
  3. Create a new application by clicking on the "New Application" button.
  4. Give your application a name and click "Create".

Step 2: Adding a Bot to Your Application

  1. After creating your application, navigate to the "Bot" tab.
  2. Click on "Add Bot".
  3. Customize your bot's username and icon if desired.
  4. Copy the bot token. This will be used to authenticate your bot with Discord.

Step 3: Inviting Your Bot to a Discord Server

  1. Go back to the "General Information" tab in your application.
  2. Copy the "Client ID".
  3. Use the following URL, replacing "CLIENT_ID" with your actual client ID:
    https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=CLIENT_ID&scope=bot
  4. Select the server you want to add the bot to and click "Authorize".

Congratulations! Your bot is now in your Discord server.
